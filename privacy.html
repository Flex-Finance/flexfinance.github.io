We’re glad you chose to visit Flex Finance site! 2.0. Purpose

This Privacy Notice explains how Flex Finance uses the personal data we collect from you when you use our website.

3.0. Scope

Our Privacy Notice applies only to our website and not to other linked websites. For example, we link to our accounts on a number of social media platforms such as Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram and YouTube. We are not responsible for the privacy policies or content on third-party websites and encourage you to read the privacy notices of other websites you visit.

4.0. What data do we collect?

Flex Finance collects the following data:

  • Personal identification data you directly provide to us such as your Name, RC Number, business name, email address, phone number.
  • Other data generated as a result of your activity. Such as your transaction data on the platform.

5.0. How do we collect your data?

We collect data and process data when you:

  • Signup on the platform.
  • Voluntarily complete a survey or provide feedback via email.

6.0. How we use the data collected

We use the personal data that we collect to communicate with you, deliver the services you signed up for, improve our service, and to present other information and business offers that may be of interest.

When you sign up on our  platform, we ask you for your RC number, business name,  name, contact telephone number and email address.

We use this information to provide  you access to the platform. We may also email you several times after you signup in order to give you product updates. We will do this based on our legitimate interest in providing accurate information.

Your data is stored on our secured database provided by Amazon Web Services.

We do not use the information you provide to make any automated decisions that might affect you.

We keep your data for as long as you continue to use the platform. Your data is kept until you asked for them to be deleted. Only transactions data are kept based on the laws and regulations guiding the retention of such data.

7.0. Lawful Bases for Processing Data

Flex Finance processes your personal data in compliance with applicable data protection laws. In doing so, we rely on the following lawful bases:

  • Consent: When you provide explicit consent for specific purposes, such as receiving marketing communications, providing you with the service.
  • Contractual Necessity: Processing is necessary to perform the contract between you and Flex Finance, including providing you access to our platform.
  • Legal Obligation: We process your data as required to comply with legal and regulatory obligations.
  • Legitimate Interests: Where processing is necessary for our legitimate interests – such as ensuring the security of our services and delivering product updates – provided these interests are not overridden by your fundamental rights and freedoms.

8.0. How we protect your information

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to prevent loss, unauthorized access, misuse, modification or disclosure of information under our control. This includes the use of encryption, access controls and other forms of security to ensure that your data is protected. We require all parties including our staff and third-parties processing data on our behalf to comply with relevant policies and guidelines to ensure that information is protected in use, when stored and during transmission.

9.0. Where we store your Information

All Personal Information you provide to us is stored on our secure servers as well as secure physical locations and cloud infrastructure (where applicable) for the purposes of providing seamless services to you and to ensure business continuity. Whenever your information is transferred to other locations, Flex Finance will take all necessary steps to ensure that your data is handled securely and in accordance with this privacy policy.

10.0. How long we store your information

We retain your data for as long as is necessary for the purpose(s) that it was collected. Storage of your data is also determined by legal, regulatory, administrative or operational requirements. We only retain information that allows us comply with legal and regulatory requests for certain data, meet business and audit requirements, respond to complaints and queries, or address disputes or claims that may arise. Data which is not retained is securely destroyed when it is identified that is no longer needed for the purposes for which it was collected.

11. Your Rights

You have the right to access, rectify, or erase your personal information, as well as the right to restrict or object to its processing. You may also have the right to data portability.

12. Age Policy

To use our services, you have to be at least 18. We do not collect and process data of individuals under the age of 18.

To comply to this policy, we ensure to verify your age when you provide us your verification details during onboarding.
